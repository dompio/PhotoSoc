Frequently Asked Questions


Can anyone join the society?

Yes, anyone who is 18 years old or more can join. Under-eighteens can join when accompanied by a parent/legal guardian.


Do I need any previous experience?

No, we aim to provide interesting content for all enthusiasts and support the development of our members regardless of their level of proficiency. Throughout the season we organise several workshops for beginners, to let everyone familiarise themselves with the basic aspects of composition, exposition and post-production.


Where and when are the weekly meetings taking place?

Our weekly meetings take place from September until April on Thursdays at 7 pm in Dundee City Council building on Lindsay Street. See location on map


Is the location accessible for the disabled?

Yes, there is a ramp for wheelchair users at the main entrance, as well as a lift inside the building.


Do I need to bring my camera to the meetings?

No, usually our weekly meetings are held in the form of a lecture/talk, however sometimes we organise practical workshops, which is always announced in advance. On these workshops even a phone camera is fine.


Why is there a summer break?

Typically, we need time to summarise the season and prepare for the next, incorporating the feedback we received and considering the demand on particular aspects of our activity.
During summer we gather on informal meetings and travel together across the country and/or abroad, depending on our members' preferences.


How much does the membership cost?

The annual membership fee is £20, however we offer a reduced fee to students, over 60's and unemployed.


Can I try it out and come to one meeting?

Yes, you are welcome to come and see how it is to attend our weekly sessions. The first event is free of charge, if you wish to come once more, then there is a £5 fee per meeting for non-members.


Do you have darkroom or studio facilities?

No, unfortunately we don't, however we can recommend a few locations nearby.


Where can I find out more?

You can contact us via email (info@ourwebsite.co.uk) or come visit us in one of our Thursday meetings.
