Welcome to XYZ Photographic Society

Who are we?

We are a group of enthusiastic people sharing a common passion. A couple of years ago we set up an organisation to support individual development and promote the beauty of our region through photography.

What do we offer?

You can benefit from our support network, workshops and seminars taking place throughout the whole year, as well as display your works at one of our exhibitions or compete with others in numerous contests.

Join us!

Join us today and contribute to the success of one of the fastest growing photography associations in the country.
